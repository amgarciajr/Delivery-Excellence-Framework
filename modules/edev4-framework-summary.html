EDEv4 + ITIL 4
ITIL: service reliability. EDEv4: client experience of that reliability. Signal Intelligence detects friction before it becomes a formal incident.
EDEv4 + PMI
PMI: project delivery. EDEv4: what the client experiences across that arc. Outcome Owner + Three Tests complement PM role and Communications Plan.
EDEv4 + SAFe
SAFe: software velocity. EDEv4: client experience of that velocity. CEES Session + Outcome Validation complement retrospective and DoD.
"ITIL, PMI, and SAFe are delivery frameworks. EDEv4 is a trust framework. You need reliable delivery before you can build trust — but reliable delivery alone does not produce trust."
