Core Operating Model

Customer Engineer Excellence Standard

The CEES Playbook strips away reliance on specific internal tools and defines the behavioral standard for technical delivery. It is the framework for operating as a Trusted Advisor rather than a reactive order-taker.

01 / The Identity Shift

Technical brilliance is the baseline. Our differentiation is how we manage customer anxiety and business risk. Engineers operating under CEES must transition their identity:

  • ✕From Order Taker: Waits for a ticket, executes the technical requirement exactly as written, and closes the loop silently.
  • ✓To Trusted Advisor: Questions the requirement to understand the business outcome, names the risks before they happen, and establishes explicit communication cadence.
